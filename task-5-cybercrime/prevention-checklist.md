### *Stay Safe Online – College Student Guide*

#### *General Safety*

*•	Use unique and distinct passwords for all accounts.*  

*•	 Enable two-factor authentication for accounts whenever possible.*  

*•	Ensure timely updates for software, browsers, and antivirus programs.*  

*•	Do not click suspicious emails or downloading unknown attachments.*  

*•	Log out of shared devices after using them.*  

*•	Be cautious of oversharing personal information on social media.*  



#### *Financial/UPI Safety*  

*•	Be cautious of the name of the merchant and UPI IDs before making any transactions.*  

*•	Do not disclose your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone.*  



#### *Reporting Channels*  

*To report incidents, you may register on the National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in*  

*For immediate support, call Helpline 1930.*
