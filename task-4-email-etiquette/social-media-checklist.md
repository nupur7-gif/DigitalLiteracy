&#x20;*Social Media Do’s and Don’ts (For College Students)*



*✅ Do’s*



* *Make sure your passwords are unique and safe.*
* *Think things through before posting them online.*
* *Make sure your profiles are professional and polite.*
* *Verify information before sharing it.*
* *Use privacy settings to control who can see your posts.*



*❌ Don’ts*



* *Never reveal sensitive or private information in public.*
* *It is forbidden to post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.*
* *Refrain from clicking on unknown messages or questionable links.*
* *Don't spread false or untrustworthy information.*
* *Refrain from engaging in cyberbullying or online harassment.*
